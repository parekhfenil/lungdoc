## Verify Your Coverage

**Before your visit, we recommend:**

1. **Call your insurance company** to verify we are in-network
2. **Check if you need a referral** from your primary care doctor
3. **Understand your copay/deductible** for specialist visits
4. **Confirm coverage** for any tests your doctor may order

**Need help?** Text or call us at (951) 737-5809 and we can help verify your coverage.

## Prior Authorization

Some procedures and treatments may require prior authorization from your insurance company before we can proceed. This includes:

- Pulmonary function testing
- Sleep studies
- Certain medications (especially biologics)
- Advanced imaging (CT scans, etc.)

**Our team handles the prior authorization process** to minimize delays in your care. This may take 3-5 business days.

## Understanding Your Bill

### What to Expect
After your visit, you may receive:
1. **An Explanation of Benefits (EOB)** from your insurance company (NOT a bill)
2. **A bill from our office** for any patient responsibility (copay, deductible, coinsurance)
