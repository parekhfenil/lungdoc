## SEO Notes for Blog

### E-E-A-T Requirements (Critical for Medical Content)
- Every article MUST have named physician authorship
- Include author credentials in byline
- Add "Medically reviewed by" if written by staff
- Include publish date and update date
- Link to authoritative sources (medical journals, .gov sites)

### Content Strategy
- Publish 2-4 articles per month minimum
- Target long-tail keywords in titles
- Update old content annually
- Share on social media
- Repurpose into patient handouts

### Schema Markup for Blog Posts
```json
{
  "@context": "https://schema.org",
  "@type": "BlogPosting",
  "headline": "[Article Title]",
  "author": {
```
