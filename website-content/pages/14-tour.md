## What to Expect at Your Visit

**First-time patients can expect:**

1. **Arrival & Check-in** - Arrive 15 minutes early to complete paperwork
2. **Insurance Verification** - Bring your insurance card and ID
3. **Vital Signs** - Our medical assistant will check your vitals
4. **Preliminary Assessment** - Review of symptoms and medical history
5. **Consultation with Your Physician** - One-on-one time with Dr. Maheshwari or Dr. Lee
6. **Diagnostic Testing** (if needed) - PFT, oxygen assessment, etc.
7. **Treatment Plan Discussion** - Understanding your diagnosis and next steps
8. **Scheduling Follow-up** - Before you leave

**Average first visit:** 45-60 minutes
