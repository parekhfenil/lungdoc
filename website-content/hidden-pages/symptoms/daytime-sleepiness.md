## Sleepiness vs. Fatigue

**Sleepiness** is the inability to stay awake (dozing off at traffic lights or during meetings). This is legally dangerous and a major health risk. It typically signals a sleep disorder like:
- Sleep Apnea
- Narcolepsy
- Insomnia
