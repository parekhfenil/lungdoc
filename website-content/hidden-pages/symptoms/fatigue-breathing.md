## Why Am I So Tired?

Fatigue combined with breathing issues often points to:
- **Sleep Apnea:** Poor sleep quality lowers oxygen levels.
- **COPD/Heart Failure:** Your body works harder to breathe, burning energy.
- **Anemia:** Less oxygen delivered to tissues.
