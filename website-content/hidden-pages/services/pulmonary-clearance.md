## Why Pulmonary Clearance?

Before undergoing surgery with general anesthesia, surgeons often require a lung evaluation to reduce the risk of complications like pneumonia or respirator dependence, especially if you have a history of smoking, asthma, or COPD.

## What We Do
- Physical Exam
- Risk Assessment
- Spirometry (if needed)
- Optimization of medications before surgery
