If you live in Corona and wake up tired despite sleeping all night, you might have sleep apnea. Common signs include:

- Loud snoring (often noticed by your partner)
- Waking up gasping or choking
- Morning headaches
- Daytime fatigue and sleepiness
- Difficulty concentrating
- Irritability
